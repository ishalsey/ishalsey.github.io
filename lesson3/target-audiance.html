Audience

The target audience is people who are going on road trips families that are going on long road trips together.

this website will be for people who want to take a trip somewhere and see the weather driving conditons on the way. So that will include family travel and Truck drivers or the companies that run them.

Scenario's

1. A young family is going to go on a family trip accross the states
2. A young couple moving to another state and worried about the weather.
3. A truck driver taking items accross the country.
4. A school trip needing to assure families that it will be a safe drive.
5. Airports tracking weather along thier flight.
